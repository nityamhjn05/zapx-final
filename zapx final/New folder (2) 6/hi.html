About Us

One of the biggest challenges that electric vehicle (EV) owners face is the lack of real-time information on station availability and functionality. This can be frustrating for EV drivers who need to charge their vehicles quickly or are traveling in unfamiliar areas. In addition, there is often no way to determine if a charging station is out of service or in use by another driver. This means that EV owners may waste time driving to a station only to find that it's not operational or already occupied.
